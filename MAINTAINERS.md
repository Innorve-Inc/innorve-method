## How to reach us

- **Code review and PR triage:** open a PR
- **Substantive feedback on the Method:** open an issue with the `framework-feedback` template
- **Trademark or brand misuse:** legal@innorve.ai
- **Code of Conduct violations:** conduct@innorve.ai
- **General questions:** hello@innorve.ai

## How maintainers are chosen

Maintainers are invited by the existing maintainer team based on demonstrated:

- Sustained good judgment in PR reviews
- Quality of writing in skill documentation
- Engagement with the Innorve Academy community
- Commitment to the open-methodology / protected-credential model

There is no application process. Invitations are extended privately.
