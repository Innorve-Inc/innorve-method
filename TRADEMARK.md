The integrity of the credential and the brand is what makes the open methodology valuable. If anyone could call themselves an Innorve Architect, the credential would mean nothing. If anyone could publish "Innorve Method v2," the methodology would fragment.

## Earning the credential

The Innorve Architect credential is earned, not claimed. To become a Certified Innorve Architect (Level 4 on the Architect Ladder), you must:

1. Complete the Innorve Academy AI-Native Bootcamp at the Scale tier or above
2. Ship a peer-reviewed capstone that demonstrates mastery of the Innorve Method
3. Pass a structured Architect exam administered by Innorve Academy
4. Have at least one real client engagement (or equivalent in-house project) reviewed under the Innorve Method

Apply at https://innorve.academy/apply.
