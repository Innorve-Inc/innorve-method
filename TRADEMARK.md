## Section 5 · Why this separation exists

The credibility of the credential is what makes the open methodology valuable. If anyone could call themselves an Innorve Architect, the credential would lose its meaning to the employers, regulators, and review boards who look for it. If anyone could publish a competing "Innorve Method v2," the methodology would fragment into incompatible variants and the standard would dissolve.

Many serious open standards work this way: the underlying material is published freely, and the body that maintains it reserves the right to certify and to evolve the canonical version. We have made the same choice deliberately, in our own words, for our own reasons.

The Apache License is the right license for this code. It is not a license for the brand. The two operate in parallel and we maintain them with equal seriousness.

## Section 7 · Earning the credential

The **Innorve Architect** designation (Level 4 on the Innorve Architect Ladder) is earned through:

1. Completion of the Innorve Academy AI-Native Bootcamp at the Scale tier or above
2. Submission and peer review of a capstone system demonstrating practical mastery of the Innorve Method
3. Successful completion of the Innorve Architect examination administered by Innorve Academy faculty
4. Documented experience on at least one real client engagement (or equivalent in-house project) reviewed under the Innorve Method

The **Innorve Fellow** designation (Level 5) is granted by invitation in recognition of substantive contribution to the Innorve Method itself — through framework refinement, published worked examples, open spec implementations adopted by others, or sustained mentorship of subsequent Architect cohorts.

Apply at [innorve.academy/apply](https://innorve.academy/apply). Renewals and re-certification follow the schedule published at [innorve.academy/method](https://innorve.academy/method).
