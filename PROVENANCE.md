# Provenance

This document declares the origin and authorship of the materials in this repository.

---

## Authorship

Every framework, skill, schema, specification, and document in this repository was authored by the engineering and education team at **Innorve, Inc.** for publication under [Innorve Academy](https://innorve.academy).

The Innorve Method (IM-01 through IM-08), the Innorve Architect Ladder (L0 through L5), the Skill Contract Schema, the Maturity Gate Model, the Capability Graph Format, and the Innorve Native Mode doctrine are original works produced by Innorve, Inc.

The methodology was distilled from the team's own production work over multiple years building AI systems for organizations operating in regulated environments. The frameworks reflect the team's judgment about what works and what fails when AI is shipped into real businesses with real auditors and real consequences.

## What this repository does NOT contain

This repository is published openly and is intentionally limited in scope. It does **not** contain:

- **Production code from any organization Innorve has worked with.** No client codebase, in whole or in part, is reproduced or referenced in this repository. No customer-specific patterns, internal tooling, or proprietary implementations are included.

- **Customer names, logos, identifiable engagement details, or anonymized-but-traceable references.** The worked examples in skills (banking, healthcare, SaaS contexts) are composite scenarios designed to illustrate framework application — not retellings of specific engagements.

- **Third-party proprietary material.** No methodology, framework, code, schema, or text from any other organization, vendor, open-source project, or commercial product is reproduced here. Where regulatory frameworks (SOC 2, HIPAA, EU AI Act, NIST AI RMF, ISO 27001, PCI DSS, GLBA) are referenced, only their publicly-available structure and named controls are mentioned, in accordance with normal professional practice.

- **Inherited skill structures or scaffolding.** The skill format used in this repository — including frontmatter conventions, section ordering, doctrine statements, artifact specifications, and quality rubrics — is original to Innorve Academy and was authored from scratch for this publication.

- **Trade secrets or confidential information** of Innorve customers, partners, or third parties.

## Independence

The Innorve Method is independent of any specific AI vendor, model provider, runtime, framework, IDE, or development environment. The methodology is intentionally tool-agnostic.

Where this repository's installation instructions reference specific tools (Claude Code, Cursor), they are named only as the runtimes that consume the publishable skill format. Innorve Academy has no commercial affiliation, sponsorship, or partnership with these tool vendors at the time of publication.

The Innorve Method does not endorse, recommend, or favor any specific:

- Foundation model or model provider
- Agent framework or orchestration library
- Vector database, retrieval system, or embedding provider
- Cloud platform or deployment target
- Observability, evaluation, or governance vendor

Examples in this repository may name specific tools by their published names for illustrative purposes. Such naming is descriptive, not promotional, and is subject to revision as the tooling landscape evolves.
