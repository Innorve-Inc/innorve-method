## Versioning policy

- **Patch** (`0.1.x`) — typo fixes, clarifications, additional examples, additional further-reading references. No skill behavior change.
- **Minor** (`0.x.0`) — new examples sections, expanded coaching flows, refined output formats. Backward-compatible.
- **Major** (`x.0.0`) — adding, splitting, or removing frameworks. Reserved for substantial methodology updates with at least 12 months of community deprecation notice.

Frameworks that get deprecated will be flagged with `[deprecated]` in the README and on https://innorve.academy/method for at least one minor version cycle (typically 6-12 months) before removal.
